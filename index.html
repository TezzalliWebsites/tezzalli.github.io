About Tezzalli




Tezzalli was founded in 2022 by Mani Agrawal with the goal of catering to consumers of the burgeoning cosmetics industry of Latin America, a market segment that we believe traditionally has been underserved by the larger cosmetics manufacturers like L'Oréal and Unilever. Historically, these manufacturers have focused on the 10th of the global population which is exclusive of those of varied racial heritages.

According to IMF estimates, the current population of Latin America is 650.75 million. This represents about 8.28% of the total global population. Considering Latin American population size and projected growth, and the continuing positive growth trend of those moving up the socioeconomic ladder into middle-class status, we firmly believe that the Latin American market represents a golden opportunity for strategic growth and development. We expect to see record gains within all cosmetic segments, particularly in sun protection and anti-aging products, and are strategically positioning Tezzalli to capitalize on these growth opportunities.

In addition to our globally sourced offerings on Tezzalli.mx, we are thrilled to announce the creation of our exclusive Tezzalli Perla cosmetics line, debuting in November of 2023 with three exceptional products on offer: a Serum, Facial Cream, and SPF 50 Sunscreen.
